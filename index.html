About The Club

Eagles Football Club, also known as the "Sky Kings", is a passionate and community-driven football club based in Enugu, Nigeria. Founded in 2027, with the belief that football is more than a game, we are committed to discovering, developing, and empowering young talents both on and off the pitch.

From U-15 academy to professional competition, we develop complete players: skilled on the ball, strong in character, and ready to make Nigeria proud. We play with heart. We train with purpose. We RISE ABOVE ALL.

Our colors of Gold, White, and Dark Red represent Success, Unity, and Passion - the values that drive us forward every day.

Our Vision

To develop elite football talent and become one of the most respected football clubs in Nigeria and Africa.

Our Mission

  • Develop Young talents
  • Promote discipline and Education
  • Compete Professionally
  • Empower both male and female footballers
